About the Similarity Methods
Jaccard Index

Measures similarity as the size of the intersection divided by the size of the union of the two sets of GO terms. A score of 1.0 means the genes share exactly the same GO terms; 0.0 means no overlap.

J(A, B) = |A ∩ B| / |A ∪ B|

Overlap Coefficient

Measures similarity as the size of the intersection divided by the size of the smaller set. This is useful when one gene has many more annotations than the other.

Overlap(A, B) = |A ∩ B| / min(|A|, |B|)
